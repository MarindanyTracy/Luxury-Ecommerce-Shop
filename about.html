Know what we are about

Hello

At Clothology we’re dedicated to offering outfits that enable all women to express themselves, helping you feel confident in your everyday.

Since 2004 we’ve offered luxurious outfits that can be stacked and styled, with everything from jewellery and hair accessories to bags and seasonal favourites.Our outfits are an effortless style solution to refresh your look - they’re the outfits that make you stand out elegantly.

We're committed to giving back and supporting the community. One of the ways we do this is working with charities that support women. We’re also constantly improving our sustainability efforts by making conscious choices with the materials we use and our production processes.

Clothology is a trusted brand for almost 2 decades now by women across the world as a stylish quality guaranteed and up-to date shop for all your clothing needs. We continue to make sure we offer the best for our clients. #clothology #beinspired #lookelegant #sassylooks

The Difference

We make

From the beginning we've worked with locals around the communities to give them sustainable independence. As a founding member of the Ethical Initiative (EI) we’ve been at the forefront of driving sustainable improvements in working conditions and livelihoods wherever we do business.

In 2010, The Clothology Trust was founded and through education, healthcare and income generation projects, the trust helps disadvantaged women and children across Africa.
